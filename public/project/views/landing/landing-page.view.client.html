Welcome to the Event Horizon

The Most Interesting Events All in One Place

Find Events!

Event Horizon is a platform for event goers to find, coordinate, and remember the events they are interested in attending. Event goers can choose to follow events and view them listed on their profile page. They can also view dynamically generated weather updates for the date of events that they view.

Coordinate with Event Planners!

Event planners can post their free and paid events here under a variety of categories allowing them to reach out to an audience looking to have a good time. Interested event goers can reach out to planners and send them direct messages and be responded to, and if planners intend on hosting multiple events, they can even allow event goers to rate and comment on their public profile.

And More...

  • Have a favorite event planner? Follow them to have access to their events instantly.

  • Enjoy the event? Direct Message the event planner to give them valuable feedback.
